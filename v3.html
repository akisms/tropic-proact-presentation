Enablement

Two audiences. Two purpose-built assets.

Prospects need to be convinced; customers need to see their own data. That's two different jobs — so two different assets.

SALES
For Sales · Prospects
Interactive Product Demo

A click-through demo (Arcade, Navattic, or Storylane) that shows Proact in action on a representative dataset. Embedded on the landing page, used by reps in discovery, and linked from outbound.

Why this one Prospects don't have data in Tropic yet. They need to see the "aha" fast — an interactive demo delivers the narrative and the product moment without scheduling a call.
Contingency · If no interactive demo tool A short pitch deck + 2-min pre-recorded Loom walkthrough. Weaker because it loses the self-serve "play with it" element — but consistent, and a rep can still walk someone through it live.
CS
For AM / CS · Customers
Savings Opportunity Report

A templated, auto-generated report that runs Proact on each customer's actual contracts and surfaces their top 5 savings opportunities. CSMs walk it through in QBRs and leave it as a deliverable.

Why this one Customers already have data in Tropic — a generic demo wastes an opportunity to use real numbers. Showing them their own dollars is a fundamentally more persuasive education moment than any walkthrough could be.
Contingency · If report template isn't ready A one-pager summarizing Proact's capabilities + benefits. Generic, but easy to leave behind after a QBR until the data-driven report ships.
Bonus

An AI chatbot trained on foundational PMM materials about Proact — messaging, copy guidelines, objection handling. Self-serve answers for the whole org, available anytime.

Tier 2
 New or significantly improved functionality. Primarily current customers — drives activation of existing accounts. Product release notes, in-app announcement, CSM enablement moment, single outbound email. A new dashboard view or expanded integration surface.
03

 Minor changes or bug fixes. Existing customers only (as needed). Changelog entry. No GTM investment. UI polish, performance fix, minor setting additions.
