Slide 07 · Go-to-Market

Three waves. One takeover.

Tier 1 launches need volume and credibility. We sequence who's doing the talking so each wave compounds on the last.

Tier 1 Launch New functionality, both current and new customers, high revenue potential.
Wave 1 · We're Talking
Own the narrative
Weeks 1–2
Landing page
Our most important owned surface — the largest canvas we have to tell the story.
Blog announcement
The redirect target for every other channel. Goes into greater depth on what Proact does and why it exists.
Email series
Meets prospects and customers in their inbox. Doubles as a reactivation path for dormant leads.
Wave 2 · Employees Talk
Humanize the story
Weeks 1–4 (overlapping)
Employee-generated content on LinkedIn
AI has made authentic, human content more valuable than ever. Done well, it creates a LinkedIn "takeover effect" that makes Proact hard to avoid — and signals thought leadership.
Wave 3 · Customers Talk
Social proof closes it
Weeks 3–5
Customer stories
Nothing speaks to the power of a feature like tangible impact seen by real customers.
Customer webinar
Brings the feature to life, lets customers discuss specifics of the use case, and lets the market ask live questions.

CS
For AM / CS
Interactive Demo
